TONELUX SUMMING MIXER

Our Tonelux summing mixer has 44 inputs and 4 sends which all get bussed into transformer-coupled stereo bus. Tonelux uses proprietary op-amps throughout the signal path, which combine the sonics of true Class A electronics with the efficiency and dynamics of a well-designed Class AB circuit. These op-amps, plus the Tonelux output transformer on the stereo master, create a sound unique to Tonelux.
